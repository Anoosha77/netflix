Help Center

Getting Started

Begin streaming your favorite content by following these steps:

  • Create your account with a valid email.
  • Verify your email and log in to your dashboard.
  • Choose a subscription plan and start watching instantly.

FAQs

Common questions answered below:

  • Q: How do I reset my password?
    A: Click “Forgot Password” on the login screen and follow the steps.
  • Q: Can I watch on multiple devices?
    A: Yes! Depending on your plan, you can stream on 1–4 devices.

Troubleshooting

Experiencing issues? Try these tips first:

  • Check your internet connection and router.
  • Clear your browser/app cache.
  • Try logging out and logging back in.
